About Us

At Asteroid Auditors we seek to increase trust in companies that base their platform on blockchain technology. Where physical complaints are difficult to carry out through transactions carried out in cryptocurrency networks. Together with our audit, we will provide the users of a platform that works with us, the data of the CEO of the same, in the event of a fraud, scam, plagiarism, among different methods of possible complaint to be made, through us. These data are encrypted by the company, and can be given to the community in the event that any of the aforementioned situations occurs.

Efficiency

Security

Support
